Skill 		(SKL): 	Increases defense, and reduces enemy defense.
Might 		(MGT): 	Increases attack damage and equip capacity.
Fortitude 	(FOR): 	Increases health, and reduces disable duration.
Will 		(WIL):	Increases arcane power and resistance.
Faith 		(FTH):	Increases mana pool, and power of faith-based spells.
